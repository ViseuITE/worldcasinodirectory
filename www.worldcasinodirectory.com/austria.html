Austria Casinos and Gambling Facts

Austria is a country in Western Europe with legal gambling. There are 106 cities with gambling facilities in Austria which have 167 legal gambling facilities available in total.

The types of gambling available in Austria are: casinos, horseracing tracks, sportsbetting parlours.

The largest gambling city in Austria is Graz with 13 gambling facilities, 13 tables games, 367 gaming, slot, and video poker machines.
The largest casino in the entire country of Austria is Casino Bregenz which is located in Bregenz. Casino Bregenz has 30 table games, 7 poker tables, 295 gaming and video poker machines.
