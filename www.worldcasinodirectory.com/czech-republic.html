Czech Republic Casinos and Gambling Facts

Czech Republic is a country in Eastern Europe with legal gambling. There are 142 cities with gambling facilities in Czech Republic which have 401 legal gambling facilities available in total.

The types of gambling available in Czech Republic are: casinos, horseracing tracks, sportsbetting parlours.

The largest gambling city in Czech Republic is Prague with 54 gambling facilities, 89 tables games, 1,177 gaming, slot, and video poker machines.
The largest casino in the entire country of Czech Republic is Grand Arena Complex which is located in Ruzyn. Grand Arena Complex has 140 table games, 400 gaming and video poker machines.
