Bulgaria Casinos and Gambling Facts

Bulgaria is a country in Eastern Europe with legal gambling. There are 101 cities with gambling facilities in Bulgaria which have 340 legal gambling facilities available in total.

The types of gambling available in Bulgaria are: casinos, sportsbetting parlours.

The largest gambling city in Bulgaria is Sofia with 76 gambling facilities, 64 tables games, 1,238 gaming, slot, and video poker machines.
The largest casino in the entire country of Bulgaria is Royale Palms Casino which is located in Sofia. Royale Palms Casino has 6 table games, 4 poker tables, 500 gaming and video poker machines.
