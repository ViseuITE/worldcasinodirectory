Savan Legend Hotel & Casino Review Jump to comments

Savan Legend Hotel & Casino is located in Savannakhet Province,Laos, near the Thai border and was the first 5-star hotel and casino entertainment destination in the country. The property opened in 2009 as part of a planned 2-stage development. The Entertainment Complex sits on 125 acres and offers first-class gaming, entertainment, and accommodations.

The hotel features 499 rooms and suites, the largest swimming pool in southern Lao, luxurious spa and massage facilities, threerestaurants, a cafe, two bars and an entertainment venue hosting live concerts and sporting events.

The mass gaming floor is nearly 5,000 square meters and features several executive lounges and VIP parlors for those who wish to do their gambling in a more private setting.

All visitors are afforded complimentary shuttle service from the airport or border. Distinguished customers enjoy private transportation from Bangkok and other areas in Thailand with complementary air charter service expected soon.

Savan Vegas Hotel & Casino is open 24 hours daily.
