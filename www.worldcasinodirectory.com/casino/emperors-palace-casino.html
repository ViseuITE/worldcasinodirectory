Emperors Palace Hotel Casino Resort Review Jump to comments

Emperors Palace Hotel Casino Convention and Entertainment Resort is located next to the O.R. Tambo International Airport in Johannesburg, South Africa. The casino spans nearly 200,000 square feet and holds almost 2,000 slots and video poker machines, 68 gaming tables and 15 live action poker tables.

There are four hotels here, each a unique accommodation experience. Amenities include a world-class spa facility, extensive dining options from quick bites and family fare to exquisite fine dining. Other entertainment venues include a 900 seat theatre and several smaller clubs.
