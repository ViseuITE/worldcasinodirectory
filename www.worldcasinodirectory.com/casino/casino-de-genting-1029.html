Resorts World Genting Review Jump to comments

Resorts World Genting is located within the Titiwangsa Mountains on the border between the states of Pahang and Selangor of Malaysia in an area known as Genting Highlands. The resort can be reached by car from Kuala Lumpur in about one hour. It sits high in the hills at 1,740 meters (5,710 ft) above sea level and can be reached from the base of the mountain by taking the 3.38 km (2.10 mile) Genting Skyway.

The project began in 1967 and the first hotel opened in 1971. There are now seven luxury hotels including Crockfords, Highlands Hotel (formerly Maxims), Genting Grand, Genting SkyWorlds Hotel, Resort Hotel, Awana, and First World Hotel, with over 10,400 rooms in total; more than 25 restaurants and cafes, several entertainment venues, indoor and outdoor theme parks, including Genting Sky World opened in 2022, a unique 18-hole golf course, bowling center, spa, convention center, and 204,514 square feet of gaming with over 3,000 slots and more than 400 gaming tables spread over Genting Casino and SkyCasino.

Theme Park

There's no lack of fun and interesting things to do and see while in Genting. For your convenience, here's a comprehensive list.

Chin Swee Caves Temple – where Chinese culture and history come alive in the form of intricate handiwork, monumental sculptures, and a colorful 9-story Pagoda.

Awana SkyWay – and amazing scenic tour above verdant hills and valleys, this is SE Asia’s longest cable car system and also its fastest, as it lifts visitors onward to the Genting Highlands Resort.

SkySymphony - Asia's first multimedia winch attraction where the atrium transforms into a spectacular visual storyteller via free-to-public performances of an extraordinary orchestra of audio, visual and motion graphics programming. Marvel as 1,001 winch balls suspended from a four-story-high ceiling.

Strawberry Leisure Farm – crouched in the beauty of the lush countryside, it's a spot where you can pick your own delicious fruit, mushrooms and organic sprouts, and complete our visit with its own cafe and souvenir shoppe.

Gohtong Jaya – situated high in the mountains, its Yik Kee Restaurant offers dining fare not to be missed, including specialties like succulent steamed wild patin in homemade sauce, followed by Durian Tarts.

Lim Goh Tong Memorial Park – an awe-inspiring picto-biographical tour of the famous Dr. Lim Goh Ton, the highly respected developer of the Genting Highlands and the creator of this world class Casino resort.

Orchid Farm – a leisurely spot to experience peace and quiet while meandering through myriads of colorful rows amidst the fragrances of full-blossoming orchids.

SeniKome Péng Hng - Located at Level 3, First World Plaza, the centre features a thematic showcase of art and cultural essences from Peninsular Malaysia’s East Coast states, Pahang, Terengganu and Kelantan.

SkyAvenue - experience a unique shopping experience at 6,000 feet with more than 100 retail options ranging from your every day needs to exquisite jewelry, timepieces, shoes and gadgets, along with extensive dining choices to meet every budget.

SkyTropolis Indoor Theme Park - Located Level 1, Skytropolis Indoor Theme Park. Over 400,000 sq ft of more than 20 family, adventure and thrill rides. First World Plaza, adjacent to SkyAvenue.

BigTop Video Games Park - old school arcade and carnival-style games. Located Level 2, Skytropolis Indoor Theme Park.

SnowWorld - European-themed winter village featuring life-like snowfalls, day-to-night sceneries, warm igloo houses, relics of ancient Roman castles and Toboggan slide for the entire family to enjoy. 

Genting Bowl - 20 lane glow in the dark bowling alley. Located Level 3, First World Plaza. 

Vision City Video Games Park - retro-style arcade featuring classic games like Pac-Man and Street Fighter as well as newer additions like Time Crisis 5. Located Lower Lobby, Genting Grand.

Ripley's Adventureland - five attractions including Ripley’s Adventureland, Jurassic Research Center, Zombie Outbreak, 4D Trick Art Museum and Infinity Room.  

Sky VR - latest in virtual reality technology multi-sensory attractions and arcade games. Located Level 2 , Skytropolis Indoor Theme Park.
