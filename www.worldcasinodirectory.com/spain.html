Spain Casinos and Gambling Facts

Spain is a country in Western Europe with legal gambling. There are 81 cities with gambling facilities in Spain which have 340 legal gambling facilities available in total.

The types of gambling available in Spain are: casinos, greyhound tracks, sportsbetting parlours.

The largest gambling city in Spain is Madrid with 56 gambling facilities, 88 tables games, 350 gaming, slot, and video poker machines.
The largest casino in the entire country of Spain is Casino Gran Madrid Torrelodones which is located in Madrid. Casino Gran Madrid Torrelodones has 38 table games, 25 poker tables, 200 gaming and video poker machines.
