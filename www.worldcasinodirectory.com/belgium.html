Belgium Casinos and Gambling Facts

Belgium is a country in Western Europe with legal gambling. There are 90 cities with gambling facilities in Belgium which have 149 legal gambling facilities available in total.

The types of gambling available in Belgium are: casinos, horseracing tracks, greyhound tracks.

The largest gambling city in Belgium is Antwerpen with 9 gambling facilities, 6 tables games, 94 gaming, slot, and video poker machines.
The largest casino in the entire country of Belgium is Grand Casino Brussels Viage which is located in Brussels. Grand Casino Brussels Viage has 39 table games, 10 poker tables, 400 gaming and video poker machines.
