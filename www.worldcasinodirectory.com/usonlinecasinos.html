Most of the games at US-facing sites, or anywhere for that matter, are slot machines. The most popular are video bonus slots. It took decades for the games to evolve from mechanical 3-reel machines in the 1800s, to electromechanical one-armed bandits in the 1960s, and then into spinning virtual reels on video displays in the 1970s. Since the late 1990s innovation has exploded in the online world and on land.

Online slots have reflected popular culture from the beginning. The games come in so many themes that it has become more meaningful to classify them by their features and bonus mechanics than by their appearances or narratives.

Nowadays many slots are created to give players an adventure, not only in storyline but in progress through various bonus mechanics. Multilevel progressive games within the game, interactive storylines, and elements of skill can all be part of spinning the reels now.

One of the first skill-based games with free spins, expanding wilds, and a progressive bonus round was Rival's Hole in Won. Whenever a player lands three or more scatter symbols, they're taken to a second screen game where the object is to putt a golf ball into the hole in as few shots as possible. Each of the nine holes gets a little bit harder to make par. The fewer strokes it takes to sink the ball, the higher the cash reward.

Long before skill-based gaming hit the slot floors of casinos in Las Vegas and Atlantic City, Microgaming released Max Damage and the Alien Attack in regulated markets online. The game is designed to look like a modern video slot but is more of an old-school arcade game like Space Invaders - with cash prizes. Instead of spinning the reels you destroy invading spacecraft to win random coin amounts.

While that was fun it wasn't the end of innovation by any stretch of the imagination. Betsoft, the originators of 3D slots, released the best of console and casino gaming so far with Max Quest in September 2018.

Up to six players enter the role-playing action/adventure RNG gambling game and traverse an underground maze, fighting off dangers in search of treasure.

the United States Legal Gambling FAQ

Which specific types of bonuses are normally available at US online casinos?

Despite the specific difficulties these online casino operators have experienced due to specific laws in the States, those operating the market offer their player pool the full range of bonus options. This means that all players will benefit from the corresponding offer – starting from No Deposit bonuses and free spins, through Welcome offers to regular Deposit-related bonuses.

Can US players benefit from a VIP Loyalty program at these sites?

Yes, the top US online casinos are also known to feature a VIP Loyalty Rewards program. These are normally based on the players’ regular activity – gameplay, deposits, withdrawals, bonus redemption. With the help of such factors, players get their specific ranking and enjoy the corresponding benefits. 
Speaking of which, most VIP programs for US players include the standard rewards – higher withdrawal limits, faster transactions, lower charges or even personalized service and assistance.

Are the games at these US online casinos proven fair, and how?

The top reputable US online casino sites listed at this page have been tested beforehand in order to ensure players that they can enjoy proven fair gameplay. While our staff performs their own tests, these top operators often hire separate audit companies to run tests on the games’ RNG and similar functionalities. Some of the companies in the business of auditing online casino games software include iTech Labs and Gaming Laboratories International among other reputable names.

What are the top US online casino banking options available?

Due to the UIGEA act mentioned above, payment processors available to US online casino player pools have experienced great difficulties over the years. However, with the legalization of the practice in certain jurisdictions, and the introduction of new banking methods, there are more than enough options to choose from.
Standard credit card processors such as American Express, Visa and MasterCard make up the bulk of the offering, as well as cash transfer services such as Western Union and MoneyGram. Bank transfers have been made rather difficult in the past, which is why they only an option at the regulated state marketplaces across the territory. Bitcoin is by far the most recommendable payment method, both for the rather impressive value of the cryptocurrency, and its extremely advantageous features – speed, security and anonymity.

Where can I file a complaint against a casino operator’s disloyal activities?

The respective licensing and regulatory bodies in the legal gambling states across the US are the best choices when it comes to reporting a suspicious or rogue operator. For example, in Nevada, the respective bodies are the Nevada Gaming Control Board and the Nevada Gaming Commission; in Pennsylvania – the Pennsylvania Gaming Control Board and so on.

Will I be subject to any taxes on my online casino winnings?

So far, US players have predominantly enjoyed the gameplay at offshore online casino sites, where their winnings weren’t subject to the taxation policies of their resident state. However, with regulation coming forward and licensing online casino operators in each jurisdiction separately, players will need to check the specific tax policy of their state and get charged accordingly.

Do US online casinos include sports betting in their gameplay offering?

Just like all other reputable online casinos, US operators can include multiple skins to their platform – poker room, sports betting, fantasy sports and even a bingo room. All you have to do is check if your chosen casino brand offers such content; finding one shouldn’t be too hard these days.

Which states do currently offer legal online gambling in the US?

The US online gambling stage is in a state of constant flux, with new states coming forward and seriously considering the legalization of online gambling in the US. Nevertheless, the first three states to legalize gambling back in 2013 when authority was officially handed over to local governments were Nevada, New Jersey and Delaware. Four years later, Pennsylvania joined in, prompting several others (West Virginia, per say) to look into the matter more closely.

Can I try the games for free before depositing real money?

Yes, most of the online casino game developers tend to build their games in demo mode and real money gameplay both. That way, players can try out the game mechanics, get a taste of the casino player traffic and generally have a better online gambling experience.

Which currencies are normally offered at these US online casinos?

Most commonly, these online casinos tend to focus on the US dollar, since it is the main currency used across the States. Players are usually welcomed to deposit in other currencies as well, as long as they don’t mind the exchange fee charged along the way. Other times, when dealing with internationally available operators, players can choose between multiple currency options, since the operator aims to meet the needs of a wider player audience.
